How to Deliver Feedback

Used for Behavior Change. For this to work effectively, you must:
1
Identify the issue

You must be very clear about the exact nature of the issue.

2
Stress the Importance

People are more likely to change when they fully understand the consequences.

3
Agree on a way forward

Try to establish a way forward that you can both agree on. Those who help develop a solution are more likely to implement it and adhere to it.

Used for Behavior Repetition. For this to work effectively, you must:
1
Be specific

If you want the other person to repeat the behavior, you must explain exactly what they did right.

2
Give feedback often

Nobody ever complained of being praised too often. Regularly reinforce your positive feedback.

3
Be sincere

Only offer positive feedback if you are sincere about it. When you are insincere, it makes the recipient doubt everything you say.
